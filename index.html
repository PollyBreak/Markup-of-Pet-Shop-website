About us

We are an elite highly professional pet store. Our history dates back to 1998 when the first store was established in Akmola. The first priority of our company is pets. We have always been caring of our pets with love from our employees. The second priority is our clients. We help everyone to choose a pet for their families taking into account various factors such as a person's budget, personality, spare time, health etc. Our assistants provide detailed consultations and support at every step even after several years after purchase. Our variety of pets is incredible. There are different cats🐈 and dogs🐕 breeds, rabbits🐕, hamsters🐹, rats🐀, chinchillas🐭, turtles🐢, birds🐤 and parrots🦜 and much more in our store.
